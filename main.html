UDEMY Courses Dataset

Exploratory Data Analysis

ABOUT The Project

It is an endeavour to look into the statistics of various courses which the popular website UDEMY provides to its Users.

Me and my team at ACM League of Codes had undertaken the task of delving what the current trend in Online Courses are amidst this Pandemic , is there an increase as compared to the previous years ? And , if so , which field/courses are the current rage amongst students. How much revenue Udemy has earned in the recent years and much more!!

Subject Analysis

  • High Concentration of Web Development and Business Finance courses
  • Least amount courses are under the Graphic Design.

Number of Paid vs. Unpaid Courses

  • Most of the offered courses are paid. Only 8.43% are Free Courses.
  • Authors prefer to get paid for the courses due to the time and resources they had invested.
  • High Concentration of Web Development and Business Finance courses
  • Least amount courses are under the Graphic Design.

Distribution of Courses by Level

  • Regardless of the Subject there is high concentration on All level and Beginner level courses.
  • Low concentration of Expert Level Courses compared to the Beginner and All Level courses .

User Preference Analysis

  • Users have a High subject preference towards Web Development and even more so if it is Free.
  • Graphic Design is the 2nd most Popular Courses followed by Business Finance.
  • Musical Instruments have higher preference than Business Finance when it offered for free.
  • Increasing more courses on Graphic Design would be profitable because of user preference.
  • Despite Business Finance having a high concentration of courses. It is not popular by users.

User Preference Based on Difficulty

  • Users have preference over All levels and Beginner Category.
  • Very Few People undertake Expert Courses maybe bacause they are generally Paid.

Revenue Made By Each Category

  • We can clearly see that even though the Number of Paid Courses of Business Finance is more than that of Web Development , Web Development still has contributed more revenue to the Company Overall because of it's larger popularity amongst users.

User Duration Preference

  • Most Users Prefer the courses to have less duration.
  • There is a linear relation between Reviews and Subscribers.

Time Series Analysis

  • The amount of Web Development courses being upload has taken a huge leap in the year 2015.
  • Web Development became the most uploaded course in 2016 surpassing Business Finance.
  • Web Development seems to a slight decline in 2014.
